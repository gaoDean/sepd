SEPD

Shanghai Electric Power Design Institute

SEPD (Shanghai Electric Power Design Institute) Australia, is the local operation of SEPD Co. Ltd., which is co-owned by its employees and POWERCHINA (Power Construction Corporation of China).

As the leading industrial giant with A-grade Engineering Certification in both design and consultation for China power network, SEPD has established a strong presence in shaping the power industry worldwide.

With its full capabilities in design, consultation and EPC project delivery internationally, SEPD is able to provide the most competitive services for the power transmission, distribution and renewable energy market.
